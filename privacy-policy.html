Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 1, 2025

Overview

TrackMyPlay is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles information when you use our music practice tracking service.

Information We DON'T Collect

TrackMyPlay is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • No Audio Recordings: We never record, store, or transmit your audio
  • No Personal Data: We don't collect names, emails, or personal information
  • No Usage Analytics: We don't track how you use the app
  • No Third-Party Tracking: No advertising networks or analytics services

How TrackMyPlay Works

All audio processing happens locally on your device:

  • Audio is analyzed in real-time for instrument detection
  • Only practice session metadata (duration, instrument type) is stored locally
  • No audio data leaves your device
  • All processing uses Apple's on-device frameworks

Local Data Storage

TrackMyPlay stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Practice session records (dates, durations, instruments)
  • App preferences and settings
  • Calibration settings for your instruments

This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Permissions

Microphone Access: Required for automatic instrument detection. Audio is processed in real-time and never stored.

Notifications: Optional permission for practice reminders and achievements.

Data Security

Since no data is transmitted or stored on external servers, your information is as secure as your device. We recommend keeping your device updated with the latest iOS security updates.

Children's Privacy

TrackMyPlay is safe for users of all ages. Since we don't collect any personal information, there are no special considerations for users under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
