TrackMyPlay Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 1, 2025

Developer Information

TrackMyPlay is developed by Fengming Zhong. Contact: fretio0719@gmail.com

Data Collection

TrackMyPlay does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.

Audio Processing

All audio recognition happens locally on your device. No recordings or raw audio are stored or shared.

Local Storage

Only practice metadata (session duration, calibration, settings) is stored locally. Uninstalling deletes all data.

Sharing

No third-party data sharing.

User Rights

As no personal data is collected, there is no data to delete or withdraw consent. Uninstalling the app deletes all data.
